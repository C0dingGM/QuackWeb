About Us

Our team was created in the 2021-22 Freight Frenzy season. In the beginning, our mentors from High Oak Robotics and senior team 16457 Working Title taught us the basics and gave us a strong platform for us to start building our own experiences. Since then, we have been actively working as a member of the FIRST community.

Our Values: Gracious Professionalism

"Competition for the sake not of destroying one another, but for the sake of bettering and improving both competitors as a result of the competition." - Woodie Flowers

Team Environment

At Quackology, we encourage the sharing of ideas and innovation. We believe that communication is the foundation of an engineering team, and an idea is the spark of a project. At the beginning and end of each meeting, our team engages in communication relating to recent problems and solutions.

Outreach

We are committed to continuously improving our community's awareness of STEM through attending various events relating to engineering, an event especially pitched to our younger generation. We host library meetings and collaborate with other organizations to inspire others to pursue the STEM Field.
